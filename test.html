WELCOME WE ARE DEVISE AND RENDER

Devise and Render associates is a partnership firm established in 2016. We indulge in a variety of works which includes architectural projects, interior designing, urban and rural planning, graphic designing, valuation and liaison work among others. Over the years we have added different spectrums in our forte, making us multi-dimensional. We venture in residential, office, religious, landscaping as well as retail scenarios.

We have always believed that the users’ comfort is the most crucial outcome of the design process. At the same time, we also consider sustainability and project costing as the key factors in our design. We value our relationships with the clients by remaining at their disposal for any queries and follow ups, which has helped us to maintain a high recurrence rate. We believe in the dictum by Larry Page: “Always deliver more than expected”

Our process

  • To Devise
  • To Render

To Devise

The primary step we take upon understanding the client’s requirement is to devise. To devise a vision, a concept, a theme that would reflect the need most appropriately. This is done in conjunction with constraint parameters like space, budget and timeline. To develop this vision, our diverse experience is brought to the palette, coupled with creativity and imagination. This is then presented in a comprehensive manner and put forth for discussion to the stakeholders until a token of satisfaction is expressed. Once the design aspects are worked out, we focus on refining the technical details. Thus the soul and the body are infused together to conceive the essence of the project.

To Render

After the vision and concept for a project is devised, the following step is to render. To render the idea into reality, to nurture the conceived project. This involves the collaboration of a multitude of craftsmen and technicians. The construction process is streamlined to ensure that the final output matches the vision. Effective process management, material selection, and supervision are some of the aspects that are emphasised during the rendering of the project. The project is handed over once we are content and assured that the output justifies the vision.
